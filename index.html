Lentil Soup with Chicken

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts or thighs (~400-500g)
  • 1 cup dried lentils (green or brown), rinsed
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 celery stalks, diced
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes (optional)
  • 6 cups chicken broth
  • 1-2 cups water (as needed)
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp ground coriander (optional)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Optional: fresh parsley, lemon wedges, Parmesan cheese

Instructions

  1. Season the chicken with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil in a large pot, then brown the chicken. Remove and set aside.
  2. In the same pot, sauté onions, carrots, and celery until soft. Add garlic and cook briefly.
  3. Stir in lentils, cumin, smoked paprika, coriander, and bay leaf. Add broth and tomatoes, then simmer for 25-30 minutes.
  4. Shred the chicken, add it to the soup, and cook for 5-10 more minutes. Adjust the seasoning and serve with your favorite toppings.
